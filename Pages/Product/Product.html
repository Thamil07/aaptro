AAPTRO Design Patent foldable plastic crate is a versatile storage and transportation solution made from plastic material that can be collapsed or folded when not in use, making it convenient for both storage and transportation.
AAPTRO Design Patent foldable plastic are versatile and find applications in industries such as agriculture, retail, logistics, fruits and vegetables , fisheries, marine, electrical and electrons , pharmaceutical ,agro-Products, automobiles , yarn & textile and more. They can be used for transporting produce, organizing warehouse inventory, and even as storage solutions in homes.
